## AE2Enhanced 1.2.0

**For**: Minecraft 1.12.2

---

### ✨ New: Hyperdimensional Storage Nexus

A brand new late-game multiblock storage structure.

- **Unlimited capacity**: No more worrying about storage limits — hoard as much as you want
- **Multi-type support**: Stores items, fluids, Mekanism gases, and Thaumcraft essentia (when corresponding mods are installed)
- **Save-safe**: Data is stored in separate files, preventing save lag and corruption no matter how much you store
- **Update-friendly**: Your stored items persist safely across mod updates
- **Stunning visuals**: A rotating holographic tesseract floats above the structure with energy particles

### ⚙️ New: Configuration Options

Adjustable via `config/ae2enhanced.cfg`:

- Auto-save interval for storage data
- Toggle holographic effects / max render distance
- Black hole damage mode (immunity for creative players, or disable damage entirely)

### 🔧 Other Improvements

- Added crafting recipes for all Hyperdimensional Storage Nexus blocks
- Completed translations for English, Simplified Chinese, and Russian
- Black holes no longer randomly teleport players to other dimensions
- Fixed holographic projection render distance calculation

---

**Requires**: Forge 14.23.5.2768+ · AE2-UEL v0.56.7+ · MixinBooter 8.9+

**Optional**: Mekanism (gas storage) · Thaumcraft + Thaumic Energistics (essentia storage)
